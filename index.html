Common questions

A great range of inflatables and party extras: bouncy castles, themed castles, castles with slides, super slides, obstacle courses, disco domes, bungee runs, gladiator rings and rodeo bull and sumo suits.

We cover the south east of Ireland including Carlow, Kilkenny, Wexford and Wicklow. That takes in Borris, Carlow Town, Bagenalstown, Tullow, Bunclody, Enniscorthy, Kilkenny City, Graiguenamanagh, New Ross and more.

Yes. Blackstairs Bouncers is fully insured and we are members of TIPE. Shoes are removed before use and every inflatable is set up and checked on delivery.

We deliver and set up in the morning and collect that evening. Payment is made on the day to whoever delivers, and there is no cancellation fee.
